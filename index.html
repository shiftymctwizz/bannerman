About

Before beginning your counselling journey with me, it is important you understand how I conduct my practice. I promote a holistic mind-body-spirit approach to healing. I identify as a transpersonal counsellor, yet I am eclectic in my therapeutic work. I will use many different counselling frameworks and a variety of interventions and techniques. I see 4 domains that require exploration in order for healing to occur (i.e., the physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual). I believe that for true transformation to transpire one must move beyond their everyday experience and embrace transcendental experiences.

These experiences can be facilitated through: Clinical Counselling Hypnotherapy, breath work, meditation, guided imagery, emotional/somatic processing, body movement, and many other non-invasive techniques. As an eclectic counsellor, I have the capacity to use a wide variety of techniques that can include, but are not limited to: Promoting catharsis, clinical hypnotherapy, psychodynamic inquiry, guided visualization, homework, and embracing the present moment and all emotions arising therein. These techniques will only be employed based on your particular needs.
